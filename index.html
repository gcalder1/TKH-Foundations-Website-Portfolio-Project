About Me

As an Ecuadorian, American with a strong background in music, education, and community service, I have honed my skills in communication, organization, and teamwork. My professional journey includes assisting with lesson planning, leading activities, and managing conflicts in educational settings, as well as volunteering to support low-income residents and diverse populations. My interest in technology grew while learning HTML and CSS at TKH Foundations, and I am passionate about using my creativity and technical skills to design user-focused web experiences. Fluent in Spanish and dedicated to continuous learning, I am excited to bring my unique blend of talents to the field of web development.
